1. The Language of Ancient Runes (50 words):
The Ancient Runes are a forgotten language etched into stone and time itself, each symbol carrying layered meanings that shift with context. Spoken only in whispers of magic, the runes can alter reality when correctly aligned, but misinterpretation can fracture memory, distort time, or awaken sealed temporal forces.

2. The Griffin, guardian of the Sky Peaks (50 words):
The Griffin of the Sky Peaks is a majestic guardian with the body of a lion and the wings and head of an eagle. It soars above the highest cliffs, protecting sacred temporal gates. Fiercely intelligent and noble, it judges travelers’ intentions, granting passage only to those deemed worthy and pure.
