Mist curled low across the fields when Elin discovered the map, tucked beneath a loose floorboard in her family’s weathered cottage. At first glance, it seemed nothing more than brittle parchment traced with fading ink—but the longer she stared, the more the lines shifted, as though alive. Paths appeared where none had been, winding toward the forbidden forest the villagers dared not name. Along its edges, tiny symbols pulsed like whispers caught in ink. Elin felt it then—a quiet pull, insistent and strange—calling her beyond the safety of home and into the secrets of the Whispering Woods.
