Elara, a young villager with bright, searching eyes, was known for her bravery and endless curiosity. While others feared the unknown, she was drawn to it. The moment she found the mysterious map, her heart raced with excitement, certain it was the beginning of an extraordinary adventure.
Shadow, a sleek wolf with silver-streaked fur and piercing amber eyes, moved like a silent guardian at Elara’s side. Fiercely loyal and keenly perceptive, he sensed danger before it came. Though wild by nature, his bond with Elara was unbreakable, forged in trust and shared courage.

Elara wandered lost, no compass to guide her through the mist
